About Darbic

Darbic focuses on guiding visitors and organizing movement during large-scale events by using ticket-based instructions, gate assignment, and arrival scheduling.

The Problem

Large-scale events often face crowd congestion, unclear gate directions, long waiting times, and difficulty in reaching the correct pickup or drop-off point. These issues can reduce safety and negatively affect the overall visitor experience.

The Solution

Darbic provides structured event guidance based on ticket information. It helps visitors view their assigned gate, recommended arrival time, pickup point, and guidance details in a clear and organized way.

How It Works

Darbic follows a simple and organized flow to help visitors move more smoothly inside large events.

1

Enter Ticket Information

The visitor enters the ticket number through the platform to access event-related guidance details.

2

Get Assignment Details

The system assigns the proper gate, pickup point, and suggested arrival time based on predefined rules.

3

Move with Less Congestion

Visitors follow the displayed instructions, which supports smoother movement and reduces crowd pressure.
