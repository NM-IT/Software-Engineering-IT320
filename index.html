About Darbic

The Problem

Large-scale events often suffer from crowd congestion, confusion at entry gates, long waiting times, and difficulty reaching the correct pickup or drop-off points. These issues can affect both visitor safety and the overall event experience.

The Solution

Darbic provides structured visitor guidance based on ticket information. It helps users view their assigned gate, recommended arrival time, and pickup point, while also supporting accessibility and smoother crowd movement.
